Django DevKit Appliance 1.3
May 21, 2011
[caption id=“attachment_853” align=“alignleft” width=“300” caption=“Django”][/caption] It’s been a while since I pimped my Django Dev Kit Appliance, mostly because I have been really busy with work, projects and my kid’s end of the school year.
