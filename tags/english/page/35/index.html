I need your help to get BillReminder translated into as many different languages as possible! Helping out Ubuntu

Helping the Belarusian and Esperanto Translations
Jan 01, 0001
[caption id=“attachment_1213” align=“alignleft” width=“214” caption=“Two New Languages”][/caption] Thanks to comments by booxter and Patrick Niedzielski, I have added support for 2 more languages to Pylyglot: Belarusian and Esperanto! So far I had been adding languages according to their translation percentage, starting from the top and slowly moving down the list, but it probably makes sense to add some of the languages that need a bit of help catching up with the other teams and provide them with a means to check their standardization.
