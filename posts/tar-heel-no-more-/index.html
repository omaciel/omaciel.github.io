Tar Heel No More

Jul 6, 2005

Once again I avoided mentioning about a very serious job lead I had with the University of North Carolina at the Chappel Hill campus, afraid of jinxing myself. Well, today I have finally recieved the final verdict after the 3-weeks long process: they decided to go with a local candidate who had a slight advantage over me (apparently some accounting and project management experience) and had just lost her job. I confess I’m a bit disappointed at the moment for it is the fourth position in a row I was genuinely interested that I see slip through my fingers… I’m going to try to put a positive spin on this and make myself believe that the best is yet to come… Kind of hard to do when you think of the previous chances (Microsoft for instance)… =/
