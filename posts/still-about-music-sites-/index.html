Still about music sites

May 24, 2008

I came across Global Pandora after checking out the anonymous tip left on my previous post. Apparently it is a site that explains how you can gain access to the services provided by Pandora in case you live outside the United States. I was not aware of this, but due to some legal mambo jambo, Pandora is only available in the US, which is a shame!

Since I do live in the United States, I didn’t try out the tip, but took the time to check out some of the other music sites listed on Global Pandora. The one the attracted my attention was AnywhereFM for its claim that you can upload your entire musical collection to their servers and listen to it from anywhere!

There’s also the option to create your own personalized “radio station” and share it with anyone. I haven’t tried it yet but will give it a spin some time later this weekend.
